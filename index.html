Methodology & definitions

Data scope & vintage

All production figures reflect 2024 closed residential transactions as reported in RealTrends Verified 2025 brokerage profiles (published April 2025). Each row corresponds to a single RealTrends Verified brokerage profile — not a franchise network total, except KW and RE/MAX which use T3 Sixty / company press release totals and are labeled accordingly.

Sales volume & sides

Volume = total dollar value of residential transactions closed during the year (not company revenue). Sides = count of buyer-side and seller-side representation; one sale may contribute 1 or 2 sides. Both are as reported to RealTrends Verified by each brokerage.

Implied average price

A derived estimate only, calculated as: Volume ÷ (Sides ÷ 2). Assumes two sides per transaction. Results vary where "sides" are defined differently across firms. Intended for relative comparison, not as an audited statistic.

Active agent count

RT verified = RealTrends "active licensed agents" from brokerage profiles. estimated = company SEC filings or press releases; definitions vary. The merged Compass + Anywhere ~340K figure is globally-reported "real estate professionals" — not comparable to active licensed agents.

Merger view

Replaces separate Compass and Anywhere Advisors rows with one combined row. Volume and sides = arithmetic sum of 2024 RealTrends brokerage profiles ($231.04B + $183.81B; 228,785 + 246,728 sides). Agent count is globally-reported "professionals" per company/media sources.

Rights & attribution

Data sourced from publicly available RealTrends Verified brokerage profiles, T3 Sixty press releases, and company SEC filings. Inman News is a sister publication within HW Media. Figures should be verified against primary sources before commercial use.

How to update on April 10 editorial guide

Step 1 — Add new data (5 min)

Find NEW_DATA near the top of the script block. Fill in each brokerage's 2025 production figures from the RealTrends Verified 2026 release. Leave null for any firms not yet published.

Step 2 — Flip the live flag (30 sec)

Change DATA_LIVE: false to DATA_LIVE: true. This activates the "What changed" tab, the YoY toggle bar, story banner, and green "2026 data live" pill in the header.

Step 3 — Review auto-generated story bullets

The story banner auto-computes the biggest volume mover, biggest sides change, largest rank jump, and any new entrants. Review for accuracy and optionally override the STORY_OVERRIDE text field for a custom headline.

Step 4 — Commit & publish

Push to GitHub. The page self-updates. The "What changed" tab becomes the story hook; the main chart and table update to show 2025 data with YoY context baked in.
